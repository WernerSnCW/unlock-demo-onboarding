# Unlock Free Version — Project Brief

## Scope
Unlock provides **due diligence snapshots on businesses offering investment opportunities**.  
- We **do not analyse or verify investment opportunities directly**.  
- We **do not offer financial advice** of any kind.  
- Core focus is **business-level due diligence** using publicly available information.  

## Free vs Paid
- **Free**: Aggregated snapshot reports based on open/public data.  
- **Paid**: Enriched deep-dive reports (with licensed/paid data sources).  

## Positioning
- Unlock is a **tool for investors**, not an advisor.  
- We help users **verify businesses quickly and responsibly**.
