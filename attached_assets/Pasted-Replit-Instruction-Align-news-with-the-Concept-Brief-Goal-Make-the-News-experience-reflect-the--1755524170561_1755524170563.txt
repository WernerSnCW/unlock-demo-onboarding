For every card, add:

AI badges row (small, muted):

Summary (show your 2–3 line summary—already present).

Sentiment: Positive / Neutral / Negative (badge).

Relevance: 0–1 → display as “Match: 83%”.

Related: If same ticker/topic appears in other items, show “Related: 2” pill; clicking expands an inline list of titles (no navigation needed).

4) Feed behaviour — filtering, dedupe, related links

Apply all Preference filters live (tickers, sectors, regions, topics, EIS/SEIS, include/exclude sources).

Dedupe: when “dedupe” is on, hide items whose titles are ≥80% similar to a visible item or that share the same relatedIds group; instead, increment that item’s “Related: +N” pill.

Hide low-value: hide items with relevance < 0.35 unless the user’s tickers are present.

Sorting: date desc, then relevance desc.

Load more: +5 items per click, with a brief spinner.

(Maps to “duplicate/low-value filtering” and “related story linking.”)
