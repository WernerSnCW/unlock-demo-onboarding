What to change on the Documents screen (spec)

Add document CTA: a first card “＋ Add document” (drag-and-drop + file picker).

Filters bar: Type, Coverage (Account/Wrapper/Asset), Period, Status (On file / Missing / Needs review), Source (Upload / Email / CSV / Live).

Buckets on the left: Statements, Contract notes, Valuations, Tax relief, Corporate actions, Property, Crypto, Other.

Tiling & pagination: grid that supports hundreds; each card shows Title, Type, Uploaded date, Coverage (what it proves), and a status chip (Used for reconcile / Unmatched).

Linking: on add, user selects what this document covers (one or more: portfolio, account/wrapper, specific asset).

“What’s missing” strip: auto-generated list of required docs (by month/quarter) with quick “Upload” links.

Reminders: per doc (monthly/quarterly/annually/at maturity).

Viewer: lightbox with page thumbnails + “Link to…” button to attach to holdings, plus a small notes field.

Bulk import: “Email to vault” address (prototype copy only) and AI Import tile (already in your sidebar) that proposes links after OCR.

Document types investors usually upload — and why

(Group them in the picker so users don’t overthink it.)

Portfolio & custodian level

Broker/Platform statements (monthly/quarterly/annual) – reconciliation of listed holdings & cash; tax summaries.

Cash statements / interest summaries – prove balances & interest (ISA cash too).

Pension/SIPP annual statements – contributions, charges, and valuation snapshots.

Annual consolidated tax certificate / dividend & interest reports – supports SA100/SA108 CGT and income tax.

Corporate action notices – splits, consolidations, rights; explains unit/cost changes.

Trade evidence

Contract notes / deal confirmations (buy/sell) – source of units, price, fees for cost basis.

Dividend/interest vouchers – income proof for tax.

Property

Title deed / Land Registry extract – ownership proof.

Valuation reports (RICS) / agent letters – current value evidence.

Mortgage statements – liability balance and interest.

Insurance policy / EPC / tenancy agreement (BTL) – risk & cash-flow evidence.

Private equity / venture

Share certificates – ownership.

Subscription agreement / cap table confirmation – terms, fully diluted context.

EIS/SEIS certificates (usually EIS3/SEIS3 for the investor) – tax relief claim evidence.

Companies House filings (SH01/CS01) – allotments and confirmations.

Crypto & digital assets

Exchange statements / CSV exports – balances & transactions period-by-period.

On-chain proofs (signed message / address snapshot / explorer export) – wallet ownership & balances.

Tax calculation report (e.g., year end) – supports CGT/income.

Alternatives & valuables

Bullion storage statements / bar lists – holdings verification.

Appraisal certificates (art, watches, etc.) – valuation evidence.

Invoices & provenance docs – cost basis and authenticity.

Vehicles & other real assets (if you keep them in register)

V5C / logbook, insurance, finance statement – ownership and liability evidence.

Estate & admin (optional but useful)

Will / letter of wishes, trust deeds, POA – for beneficiary pack.

Metadata to capture for every document (so it’s computable)

Type (from the list above).

Coverage: Portfolio / Custodian (e.g., AJ Bell) / Wrapper (ISA/SIPP/GIA) / Asset(s).

Period (from–to) or As-at date.

Source: Upload / Email / CSV / Live (auto).

Status: Used for reconcile / Unmatched / Superseded.

Strength: High (official statement/certificate), Medium (export), Low (screenshot).

Next due (for recurring statements/valuations).

Notes (free text).

File: name, size, type, pages.

How it ties back to the register

In each holding’s drawer, show Evidence with linked documents and their period/as-at.

In Reconciliation, mark a custodian month “Reconciled” only when a statement is linked and totals match.

The Completeness pill uses: metadata ✓ + evidence ✓ + valuation fresh ✓.
