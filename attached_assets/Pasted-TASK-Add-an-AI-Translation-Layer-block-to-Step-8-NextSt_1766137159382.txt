GOAL: Provide a short, plain-English explanation without drifting into advice or predictions.

NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
1) The deterministic summary remains the source of truth. AI may only rephrase it.
2) AI output must never include:
   - “should”, “recommend”, “buy”, “sell”, “allocate”, “rebalance”, “increase”, “decrease”, “switch”
   - “guarantee”, “will”, “expect”, “predict”, “outperform”, “return”, “alpha”
   - any security/holding names or tickers
3) AI output must always include (verbatim) a compliance line:
   “Illustrative only. Not financial advice.”
4) AI output is strictly informational and must not introduce new facts, thresholds, or numbers.
5) If the model violates rules, do NOT display the output. Show a safe fallback message instead.

B) Input to AI (Deterministic Payload)
