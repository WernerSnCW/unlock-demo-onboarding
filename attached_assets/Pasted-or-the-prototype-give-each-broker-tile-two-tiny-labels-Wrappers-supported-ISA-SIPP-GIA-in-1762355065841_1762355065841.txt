How to render this in the UI

Keep your list exactly like your mock (“Search brokers…”) but add a tiny connection pill on each card:

Live (read-only) — green plug icon.

Variable / pilot — amber plug icon (tooltip: “Coverage varies by provider / aggregator”).

Blocked — red plug with strike.

CSV / Statement — grey document icon.

Keep the wrappers line (e.g., “ISA • SIPP • GIA”) under the broker name.

Tapping a Live or Variable card takes the user to the OAuth/consent step.
Tapping CSV / Statement opens the CSV/statement upload flow.

Notes
• Aggregator coverage changes frequently and often depends on specific account types. The labels above are safe defaults for a demo; you can update them as soon as a partner confirms a working feed.
• Where we’ve marked “CSV”, we still support high-quality onboarding by parsing broker statements/contract notes into holdings and cost basis.
