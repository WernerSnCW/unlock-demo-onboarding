3) UI spec
A) CommunityPanel (card)

Header: “Community”

Sections:

SyndicateInterest

Big % label (e.g., 89%) + progress bar.

Mini metrics row: Followers, Lead investors, Avg cheque.

Tiny sparkline from interest.history.

Tooltip on bar: “% of investors short-listing this business for potential group investment.”

QuestionsFeed

Controls row:

Target filter: All · To Lead · To Company · To Community

Sort: Top · New · Unanswered

Search box (client-side filter over body/answers)

List of QuestionCards (paginated 5 per page).

Actions row (sticky foot at card bottom on desktop):

❤️ Follow (toggle company follow)

❓ Ask a Question (opens modal)

💬 Join Discussion → /syndication (stub)

B) QuestionCard

Badges: Target (To Lead, To Company, Community) — coloured chips.

Body (2–3 lines, expandable).

Meta: upvotes, followers, createdAt (relative time).

Buttons: ▲ Upvote (toggle), 🔔 Follow question (toggle).

Answers list (collapsed if >1):

Each AnswerItem shows avatar placeholder, source badge (Company – Verified, Lead Investor, Peer), body, time.

“Show X more answers” link when collapsed.

Status tag: Answered / Open.

Empty state: “No questions yet. Be the first to ask the lead, company, or community.”

C) AskQuestionModal

Fields:

Route to: Lead investor | Company | Community (radio)

Your question (textarea, min 10 chars)

Footer:

Primary: Submit (stub) → appends to local state with status:"open" and 0 upvotes.

Legal line: “Public Q&A. Keep it professional; no personal data.”

4) Behaviours

Interest bar animation: tween to interestPct on mount.

Upvote: client-side toggle; increment/decrement upvotes.

Follow question: toggle followers count and show toast “You’ll be notified when this question is answered (prototype).”

Filters/sort:

Target: filter by target.

Sort Top: by upvotes desc, then createdAt desc.

New: by createdAt desc.

Unanswered: status === "open" first, then createdAt desc.

Pagination: 5 per page with “Load more”.

Accessibility:

All controls keyboard reachable; visible focus ring.

aria-label on target chips, upvote, follow.

Announce toasts with aria-live="polite".

5) Copy (ready to paste)

Syndicate tooltip: “Shows how many investors viewing this company have short-listed it for potential group investment.”

Target badges:

To Lead → Questions about thesis, terms, diligence rationale.

To Company → Operational, product, or financial details.

Community → Peer opinions and comparisons.

Verified company response badge: “Verified Company Response”

Empty feed: “No questions match your filters. Try ‘All’ or ask a new one.”

6) Styling (tokens only)

Card radius 12px; soft shadow token.

Progress bar uses accent token; risk-colours never used here.

Badges: small, pill, token background (lead=accent; company=teal; community=muted).

Spacing rhythm 12/16/24.

7) Acceptance criteria (DoD)

CommunityPanel renders interest bar + metrics + sparkline, questions feed with filter/sort/search, and action buttons.

AskQuestionModal routes new questions to selected target and appends to list (stub).

QuestionCard shows target badge, status, upvote & follow toggles, answers with source badges; verified company answers display a “Verified” chip.

Filters (target) and Sort (Top/New/Unanswered) work together; pagination loads more.

Syndicate interest tooltip and copy present; animations smooth; toasts on follow/upvote.

All UI uses CSS variables; keyboard & screen reader usable; no console errors.
