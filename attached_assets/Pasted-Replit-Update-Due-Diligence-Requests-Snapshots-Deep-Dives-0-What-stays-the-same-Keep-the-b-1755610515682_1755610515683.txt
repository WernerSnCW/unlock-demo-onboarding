4) State & “fake worker”

Implement a tiny job engine in dueStore.ts:

Persists requests to localStorage (key unlock_due_requests_v1).

createRequest(payload) → pushes a new request with status:"queued", progress:0, sla:"fast" for snapshots, "extended" for deep dives.

Timer progression (front-end only):

Snapshots (sla:"fast"): 3–10 s → processing, then tick to 100% in ~45 s, then completed (90% chance) or failed (10%).

Deep dives (sla:"extended"): 10–20 s → processing, tick slowly (show “ETA hours” hint), complete after ~90 s in prototype.

Provide store selectors: useRequests(), useRequestById(id), useRecentRequests(limit).
