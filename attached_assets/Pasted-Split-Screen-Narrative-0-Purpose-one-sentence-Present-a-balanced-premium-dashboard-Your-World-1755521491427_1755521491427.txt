Split-Screen Narrative)
0) Purpose (one sentence)

Present a balanced, premium dashboard: Your World (profile, watchlist, preferences) on the left, and The Market (curated news & insights) on the right — clear, calm, and action-oriented. Free plan; no advice.

1) Layout & responsive grid

Desktop (≥1280px):

Two equal columns within a centred container (max-width 1280px, side padding 24px):

Left column (Your World) width ≈ 48–50%.

Right column (The Market) width ≈ 50–52%.

Row gap: 24px. Column gap: 32px.

Section order (top→bottom):

Left: Welcome Panel → Alerts & Preferences → Watchlist → Upgrade CTA.

Right: Newsletter Controls (sticky under navbar) → News & Insights Feed → Load more.

Tablet (768–1279px):

Still two columns, but reduce column gap to 24px; make right column slightly wider (≈56%) so news lines wrap elegantly.

Mobile (<768px):

Single column in this order: Welcome → Alerts & Preferences → Newsletter Controls → News Feed → Watchlist → Upgrade.

Sticky elements:

Keep Newsletter Controls bar sticky at the top of the right column (not the entire page) with subtle shadow.

2) Visual rhythm & tokens (no hard-coded colours)

Spacing scale: 12 / 16 / 24 only.

Card padding: 16–20px; card radius: 12px; shadow: soft (token-based).

Typography scale (desktop):

Page section titles: 18–20px, semibold.

Card titles: 16–17px, semibold.

Meta & chips: 12–13px, medium.

Use tokens for all colours, borders, shadows, radii, focus ring. Accent only for interactive elements (links, primary CTAs, active chips).

3) Left Column — “Your World”
3.1 Welcome Panel (top, full width)

Content:

Heading: “Welcome back, {FirstName}.”

Compact chips (max 3): Investor Type, Risk, Top Sector.

Small link: “Edit profile”.

Footnote (muted): “Free plan: Snapshot only. No advice.”

Behaviour:

If no profile: show “Improve your feed — complete onboarding (2 min)” with an action link (non-functional).

3.2 Alerts & Preferences (below welcome)

Goal: A mini summary + quick edit.

Row 1: Newsletter frequency (Daily / Weekly / Monthly) — segmented control (mirrors global setting).

Row 2: Channels (multi-select chips): In-app, Email, WhatsApp (WhatsApp shows small “verify number soon” note if selected).

Toast on change: “Preferences saved”.

3.3 Business Watchlist (limit to 3 items)

Each row:

Name (14–15px, semibold) with ✅ Verified inline if verified=true.

Sector (12px, muted) under name.

Right side: Peers X% pill + Open Snapshot (quiet button).

Footer link: “View all businesses →”.

Empty state: “No saved businesses yet. Add from News or the Index.”

3.4 Upgrade CTA (calm, premium)

Ribbon badge: Premium (small, top-right).

Title: Deep-dive due diligence

Inline bullets (muted): Director checks • Filing timeline • Web footprint • Peer Q&A

Primary button: Upgrade to Pro (inert).

Footnote: Keep free access to Snapshot. No advice; businesses only.

4) Right Column — “The Market”
4.1 Newsletter Controls (sticky bar under navbar)

Frequency (Daily / Weekly / Monthly) — segmented control.

WhatsApp alerts toggle (on/off).

When changed, updates the summary in Alerts & Preferences (left column) so both stay in sync. Toast “Preferences saved”.

4.2 News & Insights Feed

Data: from mocks (news items + “insight” items).
Card layout (desktop):

Left: 56×56 image (rounded).

Right: content stack:

Headline (16–17px, semibold; 2-line clamp).

Meta line: Source + relative date (12–13px, muted).

Summary: 2–3 lines.

Tags row: show max 3 chips; if more, add a “+N” chip.

Actions row (quiet): Save / Add to Watchlist / Share / Ask AI (inserts title into assistant input).

Match badge (if relevanceScore ≥ 0.7): small success-toned “High match”, right-aligned in headline row.

Sorting & paging:

Sort by dateISO desc; secondary by relevance.

Show initial 6–8 items; “Load more” appends 4–6. Fake a 600ms spinner for feedback.

States:

Skeletons: 3 cards on initial load & after filter changes.

Empty: “No items match your filters. Try Weekly or clear filters.”

5) Micro-interactions & feedback

Card hover: subtle lift (shadow token) + 1px translateY(−1).

Focus ring: visible, token-coloured, around segment buttons, toggles, links, and cards.

Chip overflow: tags beyond 3 collapse into a +N chip; clicking +N expands inline (in place, no modal).

All preference changes trigger a non-blocking toast (3–4s, bottom).

6) Accessibility (AA)

Keyboard navigation: natural tab order left-to-right, top-to-bottom; sticky controls are reachable and not skipped.

All actionable icons have aria-label; informative icons (e.g., “Why this?”) have tooltips and aria-describedby.

Images have alt text, or aria-hidden="true" if decorative.

Contrast: verify body text and chips meet AA in both light & dark schemes.
