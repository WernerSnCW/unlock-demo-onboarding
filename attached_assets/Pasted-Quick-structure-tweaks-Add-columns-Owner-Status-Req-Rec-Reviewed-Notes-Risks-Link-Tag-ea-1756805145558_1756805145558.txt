Expanded checklist (UK-centric)
1) Financial

Audited/statutory accounts (last 3 years) (or unaudited if applicable).

Monthly management accounts (P&L, BS, CF) – last 12–24 months + YTD.

Forecast/budget (18–24 months) with assumptions workbook and hiring plan.

Bank statements (last 12 months).

Aged debtors/creditors reports.

Revenue breakdowns (by product, customer, channel, geography).

Unit economics (CAC, LTV, payback, churn/retention, ARPA/ARR/MRR for SaaS).

Tax filings & positions (CT600, VAT, PAYE/NI), R&D claims and HMRC correspondence.

Debt & grants schedule (terms, covenants, security/charges).

Related-party transactions summary.

Cap table (fully diluted) incl. option pool, vesting, ASAs/SAFEs/convertibles, warrants.

Any valuations (e.g., 409A/EMI valuation) and waterfall for this round.

2) Legal & Compliance

Certificate of incorporation & current Articles (with history of amendments).

Shareholders’/Subscription agreements, Board minutes/resolutions for prior rounds.

Companies House filings (PSC register, confirmation statements, charges).

Material contracts register (customer/supplier > £25k or > 12 months, exclusivity, MFN).

Licences/permits (e.g., FCA, MHRA) or confirmation not required.

Employment contracts (key staff), contractor agreements, IP assignment deeds.

Option scheme docs (EMI rules, grants, vesting, leavers).

IP portfolio: trademarks/patents/registrations, domains, open-source licence audit.

Data protection: Privacy Policy, RoPA, DPIAs, DPA templates, ICO registration.

Insurance: D&O, PI, Cyber, EL/PL – schedules & claims history.

Litigation/claims and dispute log (actual or threatened).

Website terms of use/SaaS terms and SLAs.

3) Business & Commercial

Business model & pricing, Go-to-Market plan, market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM).

Competitor map with differentiators and switching costs.

Customer list & concentration (top 20 with ARR/contract dates).

CRM exports (pipeline, win/loss, sales cycle, conversion rates).

Cohort & retention analyses, NPS, churn reasons, case studies/references.

Partnerships & distribution agreements; key dependencies.

Roadmap (next 12–18 months) and milestones.

4) People & HR

Org chart (current & post-raise), hiring plan.

Key person summaries/CVs, availability/notice periods, restrictive covenants.

Compensation & options table (salary, bonus, equity %, vesting).

HR policies (handbook, disciplinary/grievance, whistleblowing, DEI, remote working).

Right-to-work and background-check policy (summary).

5) Technology & Security (for software/AI businesses)

System architecture diagram, data flows, third-party services list.

Code repositories (read-only access or snapshot), branching/release process.

Security policies (access control, key management), BCP/DR.

Pen-test/VAPT reports and remediation logs; vulnerability management.

Uptime/incident history and monitoring.

Data governance (retention, lawful bases, DSR handling).

Model cards/training data provenance (if using AI), licences for datasets.

6) EIS/SEIS Specific (UK)

Advance Assurance letter (and application pack).

EIS/SEIS1/2/3 forms status; amounts already raised and remaining headroom.

Eligibility evidence: company age test, gross assets, employee count, UK PE.

Excluded activities confirmation; state aid received (incl. de minimis).

KIC status (if applicable) with supporting calculations.

ASA/Convertible terms confirming EIS-compatibility.

Use of proceeds mapped to growth/R&D (aligns with risk-to-capital).

7) Governance & Reporting

Board pack example, KPIs/OKRs, risk register, information rights.

ESG/Modern Slavery statements (if any) or policy one-pager.

8) Transaction Documents (for the round)

Term sheet, investment agreements, disclosure letter, warranties schedule.

CP checklist (bank accounts, new Articles, board/SH resolutions).

Pro-forma cap table post-money (incl. option refresh).

Wording/clarity fixes on your current items

“Management accounts (latest)” → “Management accounts (monthly, last 12–24 months + YTD)”

“Cash flow projections” → “Integrated 3-statement forecast (18–24 months) with assumptions”

“Share agreements” → “Shareholders’ & subscription agreements; option scheme rules; option/ASA/SAFE registers”

“IP registrations” → “IP portfolio & assignments (registered + unregistered) and OSS audit”

“Competitor overview” → “Competitor map with pricing and differentiation”

“Team CVs” → “Key person CVs + org chart + options/comp summary”

Optional sector add-ons

Fintech: safeguarding, CASS, AML/CTF policies, complaints log.

Health/Medtech: clinical safety case, DCB0129/0160, regulatory pathway.

Hardware: BOM, certifications (CE/UKCA), supply chain/QA.
